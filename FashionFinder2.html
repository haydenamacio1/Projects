What is Fashion Finders?

I designed Fashion Finders because clothes, fashion, and the fashion industry has always been something that interests me and others as well. As we all know everyone in the world has their type of style when it comes to what they wear. What this site is designed for is to make it faster and easier for people to find their desired style of clothing. Ranging from various clothing sites that serve many of the different styles and categories of todays fashion.
